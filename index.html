FAQ

What is a durable loan

A durable loanis a loan extended to you for the purchase of electronics and gadgets such as smartphones, washing machines, refrigerators, TVs, etc

What is the process for availing the loan?

Please check out the ‘How to apply’ section for more details.

What are the tenors for which I can avail of the loan?

With the EMI Network, you can avail flexible tenor options ranging from 8 to 24 months.

What is the documentation required for your EMI Card?

You need to provide a valid income document, photo ID proof, residence proof and an ECS mandate. Depending on the credit program you opt for, you may be required to give some minimal additional document(s). For more details, please see the documents section within the ‘Eligibility’ section.

What is the interest and processing fees charged for the loan?

Bajaj Finserv EMI Network offersfinancing without charging any interest. These offers are available on a range of products. For products that are not covered under Durable Finance, you will be charged nominal interest as per the prevailing company interest rate and policy. A nominal processing fee is charged depending on the special offers running at the time.

How does Bajaj Finserv offer durable finance at rate to customers?

Bajaj Finservdoes not charge any interest to customers. You may be only asked to pay an upfront instalment (as applicable) for the amount financed and a nominal processing fee. The manufacturer bears an annualized rate of interest based on risk gradation, cost of funds, margin & risk premium. There are no hidden charges whatsoever.

Whom do I contact in case of any further queries regarding the loan? Where can I get this loan?

You can walk into our partner stores and connect with our in-store executives. To find out our dealer partner store nearest to you, simply fill in your location detail in our ‘Find the nearest store’ section.

Can I foreclose the loan, and what is the procedure for the same?

Yes, you can foreclose after 6 months from the loan disbursement date. Just walk into one of our branches or contact us on wecare@bajajfinserv.inand we will be happy to help you.

Are there any charges for foreclosing the loan?

There are absolutely no charges or penalty for foreclosing your loan.
